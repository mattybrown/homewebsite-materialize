All food made on site. All eggs used at cafe home are free range and sourced locally. Many of the dishes on our menu are suitable for those with food allergies - especially wheat, gluten and dairy intolerances. While cafe home will make every effort to ensure our food be 100% free of allergens, it cannot be guaranteed as we do not have a dedicated gluten free cooking area. Let your waitperson know and they will discuss your needs with the chef.
